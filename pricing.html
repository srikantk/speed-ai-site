Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. Speed PDF's core editing features — view, merge, split, annotate, sign — will always be free. We'll always have a generous free tier.

How does AI work without internet?

We use quantized open-source models that run directly on your device's CPU or GPU. No data is ever sent to any server.

What hardware do I need for AI features?

AI features work on any modern Windows laptop or desktop from the past 5 years. A GPU speeds things up but is not required.

Will there be a Mac or Linux version?

Yes — macOS and Linux versions are on our roadmap. Windows is our first platform because that's where the Preview equivalent gap is biggest.

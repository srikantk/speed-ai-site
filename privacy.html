Privacy

Privacy should be plain.

Speed PDF is being built for local, everyday document work. The waitlist collects your email so Speed AI can send launch updates and the Windows V1 download when ready.

Current website data

The waitlist form sends your email to a Speed AI Google Form. We use it for Speed PDF launch communication.

Feedback data

If you choose to submit your email with feedback in Speed PDF, your email address and feedback are sent to Speed AI systems and may be shared as required to respond to you.

Feedback submissions may also include app metadata such as the local feedback client ID, app version, and operating system to help diagnose product issues.

Product principle

Core PDF editing should happen locally where practical. Future optional AI features will be described separately before launch.

Contact

Use the email address or contact channel Speed AI provides with the public launch to request removal from the waitlist.
